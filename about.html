About Me

My Story

My background in clinical social work and special education has given me real life exposure to the very real needs of the very real people that companies and organizations strive to serve.

Using the principles or design thinking, I take a human centered design approach to my experience design process. Whether I am working on a tech platform, enterprise software, a human service or medical product, my process always revolves around the basic tenets of design research. It is most important for me to know the people who will be interacting with and benefiting from the final design and involving them in the design process as early as possible. I believe the keys to great design are strong user research, creative ideation and iteration, iteration, iteration! By working in harmony with a multidisciplinary design team and combining my skills in design research, prototyping and product management, I am able to create experiences that proudly and effectively serve their audiences.
